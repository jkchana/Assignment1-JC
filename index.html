My Digital Art

When I am creating digital art I use my sister's iPad and Pencil as my digital canvas and use the Sketch app. At a young age I have always enjoyed painting, drawing, and sketching so illustrating is exciting for me because it's different.

During the summer months my family and I enjoy taking road trips, especially through British Columbia. We have not only explored Canada but have road tripped into the United States of America as well. Visiting places like Seattle,WA Portland,OR Sacramento,CA and San Jose,CA. Throughout the years I have been able to capture many moments with my Family and we have visited cities and locations in both British Columbia and Alberta. Road trips, visiting parks in BC, and nature has been a great way for me to practice my photography and gain inspiration for Digital Art.

Adventures

1. My Family and I travelled from British Columbia to Alberta. visiting destinations like Revelstoke, Banff, Calgary and Drumheller.

2. My Family and I explored Victoria for the 3rd time.

3. My Family and I travelled once again from British Columbia to Alberta. visiting Golden, Jasper, Banff, and Revelstoke.

4. My Family and I drove from Port Alberni, to Ucluelet, and then to Tofino.
